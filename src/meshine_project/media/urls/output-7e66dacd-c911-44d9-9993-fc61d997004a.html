That depends on what you consider Exotic.

I am Canadian and I lived up in the Arctic and my parents knew people who hunted.

I have therefore had the opportunity to eat Moose and Elk. Delicious, rich flavour like really good beef, the texture was ‘grainy’ in a way that I still find hard to describe but really quite pleasant.

I have had wild duck and duckling, whatever they had been feeding on gave them a sort of resinous flavour like pine needles but subtle. The experience was slightly ruined by biting down on some birdshot.
